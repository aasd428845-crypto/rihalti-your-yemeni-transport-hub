الخطوة 1 — تنظيف App.tsx (src/App.tsx):
احذف هذه الـ imports كاملاً من الملف (كل سطر يذكر أياً منها):
- SupplierDashboard, SupplierBookings, SupplierFinance, SupplierMessages
- SupplierOrderDetails, SupplierPayments, SupplierPromotions
- SupplierSettings, SupplierShipments, SupplierTrips, PartnerPaymentSettings
- DriverDashboard, DriverActiveRide, DriverRideDetails, DriverProfile
- DriverHistory, DriverEarnings, DriverSettings, DriverNotificationsPage

- RideRequestPage, RideDetailsPage, RideTrackingPage
- PartnerProfilePage, PublicPartnerProfilePage
- SupplierLayout, DriverLayout (taxi - الـ layout للـ driver العادي ليس DeliveryDriver)

احذف هذه الـ Routes من JSX:
- كل <Route> داخل SupplierLayout
- كل <Route> داخل DriverLayout (taxi)
- <Route path="/trips" ...>
- <Route path="/ride/request" ...>
- <Route path="/ride/:id" ...>
- <Route path="/ride/track/:id" ...>
